About Food Safety Recall

This site was launched to help the consumer check on food recall information while in the store. Using the simple search page, the site will return the information you requested and assist in keeping you safe while shopping.

What you should know about food recalls:

To identify if a recalled product is in your home, match identifying marks on the product with the recall notice details such as:

  • Product name
  • Brand
  • Container codes
  • Container size

The recall of one product does not mean all forms of that product are a potential problem.

Info above gathered from recallbasics.org.
